Personal Information

When a User registers with Ticketsloth, they create their own profile. User profile information, as well as a User's name, email, phone number, social media information, photo and other personal information, may be displayed to users of the Ticketsloth service to enable you to connect with people on the Service.

  • User Provided Information: For visitors to the website and registered Users, we log standard technical information, such as an IP address. If a User chooses to register with Ticketsloth, the User will need to provide a valid email address and phone number. The User will also be required to select a Username and password. Ticketsloth may collect other personal information from time to time, such as when Users provide feedback, comments or communicate with others through the Service.
  • Cookies: Ticketsloth uses cookies, small text files containing a string of characters, to operate its website and provide a personalized User experience. Ticketsloth uses both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie is temporary and disappears after you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain after a User closes the particular web browser used to access the Service. Persistent cookies may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the site. Persistent cookies can be removed by using the mechanisms in a web browser. A browser's help function should explain this process. Users have the option to set their web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of the Ticketsloth service may not function properly if the ability to accept cookies is disabled.
  • Log File Information: When you use the Ticketsloth service, our servers automatically record certain information that a User's web browser sends whenever they visit the website.   • Address Book Email Addresses: If a User chooses to use the Service's invitation application to contact and invite one of your personal contacts or friends (a "Friend") to the Service site, Ticketsloth will ask the User for information needed to send the invitation, such as an email address and password. Ticketsloth will automatically send your Friend an email or instant message inviting them to visit the site. Ticketsloth stores this information to send this one-time invitation, to register a Friend connection if a User's invitation is accepted, and to track the success of our referral program.

Policy for Young People's Privacy

Ticketsloth complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act and will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18. If you are under 18, please do not attempt to register for Ticketsloth or send any information about yourself to Ticketsloth, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 18 may provide any personal information to or on Ticketsloth. In the event that Ticketsloth learns that we have collected personal information from a child under age 18 without verification of parental consent, Ticketsloth will attempt to delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that Ticketsloth might have any information from or about a child under 18, please contact us at info@Ticketsloth.com.

Ticketsloth recommends that young people between the ages of 13 and 18 ask their parents for permission before sending any information about themselves to anyone over the Internet. Ticketsloth strongly encourages parents to supervise children when online. Ticketsloth therefore recommend that parental control tools be put in place.
