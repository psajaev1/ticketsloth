How It Works

  1. Search for the ticket you want to see, if you can't find it just post a looking for listing.
  2. If you found a listing, message the user or just commmit to the transaction to secure your spot. Or wait for someone to respond to your listing.
  3. Once both you and the seller/buyer commited to a transaction, you can coordinate to meet up or transact online.
  4. Don't forget to leave a reivew for your partner, this way both of you can build reputations on the site and ensure a safe and trustworthy experience.
